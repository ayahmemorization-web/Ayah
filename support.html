Frequently asked

Is my progress saved?
Yes — your plans, progress, and review history are stored on your device.

Does recitation checking need internet?
The optional recitation check sends a short audio clip to our service to analyze, so it needs a connection. Reading, drilling, and reviewing all work offline.

How do subscriptions work?
Subscriptions are billed through your Apple account and can be managed or cancelled anytime in your iPhone Settings under your Apple ID → Subscriptions.

I can't afford a subscription.
Ayah offers a free option for those who genuinely cannot afford it. You can find it on the subscription screen.
